## Project

**Name:** Portfolio — Pristian Budi Dharmawan

**One-line description:** A personal portfolio website that showcases professional background, experiences, technical stack, and projects with scroll-driven animations.

**Status:** Active development

---

## Users

**Primary users:** Recruiters and hiring managers evaluating Pristian as a candidate; professional contacts and peers exploring his background.

**Secondary users:** Potential clients or collaborators assessing technical competence and project history.

---

## Problem

Pristian needs a professional online presence that communicates his identity, skills, and experience in a memorable, well-crafted way — beyond a static CV or LinkedIn profile.

---

## Key Use Cases

1. Visitor scrolls through the 8-section landing page to get a full picture of Pristian's background
2. Visitor explores the experience timeline to understand career history and growth
3. Visitor browses the project showcase to see real work and technical breadth
4. Visitor reviews the tech stack section to assess skill coverage
5. Visitor finds and accesses the CV / resume download link

---

## Out of Scope

- CMS or admin interface for content management
- Blog or long-form writing section
- Contact form with backend processing
- User authentication or accounts
- Multi-language support
- Analytics dashboard
